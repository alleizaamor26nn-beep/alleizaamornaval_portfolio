About Me

Hi I'm Alleiza, a student with hands-on experience in developing systems for academic purposes. I have worked on several projects, including a Violation Record Management System using C# in Visual Studio 2012, where I collaborated with a partner, and a Point of Sale System developed using VB.NET, also with a teammate. I have also independently created a Mini Library System using VB.NET that allows users to borrow books, view available books, and perform basic CRUD operations.

I have basic experience working with databases, mainly using MySQL through phpMyAdmin and XAMPP (localhost), and I understand how table relationships work and how data flows within a system. I have also tried developing a PC Inventory System website using HTML and CSS, as well as creating simple games using C# and RPG Maker. In addition, I am familiar with Figma for creating UI prototypes and have used Adobe Photoshop for school-related activities. I am also a NC II holder in Computer Systems Servicing, which gave me foundational knowledge in computer hardware and troubleshooting.

Although most of my projects were created for school requirements, they helped me discover my strengths. I learned that I am a creative thinker who often comes up with ideas to further improve a system, even when the main concept is already defined. I enjoy designing interfaces and improving user experience, and I consider myself more inclined toward front-end development, while still understanding the logic behind system and database design. I am eager to continue learning, improving my skills, and gaining real-world experience.
