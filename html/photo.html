Basic Google sheet Project

I also developed a ready-made Content Calendar and Task Manager using Google Sheets, designed to help plan, schedule, and manage social media posts efficiently. The calendar allows users to organize content by platform, post type, image idea, caption, and Canva design links, making content creation and planning faster and more streamlined. It includes status checkboxes to track the progress of each post from planned, drafted, and reviewed, to completed ensuring nothing is missed. This tool is highly customizable, easy to use, and can serve as a practical solution for content creators or marketing teams to maintain consistency and organization across multiple platforms.
